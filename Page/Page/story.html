Josh & Mike

REBEL8 remains independently owned and operated. Our reach is global

REBEL8 began at 833 Divisadero Street, a less than two hundred square foot studio apartment. On the wall is the art for the first catalog. And just below that is a warehouse listing for daily inspiration. Three years later, REBEL8 moved into that exact spot. Beneath the loft bed is the inventory. Each morning the boxes went outside into the alley to ship orders. It worked for awhile, until it rained. The bike with basket was used to slang tees around the city. The kitchen was mainly used for box storage. This is what starting a brand in 2003 with five-hundred bucks looks like.
